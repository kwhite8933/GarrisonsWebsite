Newly renovated in 2011, Garrison's offers both a relaxing, family-friendly restaurant and a fun, welcoming lounge.

In our restaurant, we pride ourselves in serving high quality food at exceptional prices. We feature delicious steaks, fresh seafood, Italian specialties and many traditional American classics. Best of all, our servers will make sure your dining experience is a pleasure.

In our lounge, you will find 4 widescreen televisions as well as Keno/lottery screens. We have live entertainment on Thursday, Friday and Saturday. Our bartenders are friendly and pour the best drinks around, but we also feature a wide selection of beers and wines.

Come in today and you'll quickly become one of our loyal regulars!
